Things to Do

Hiking Volcan Mountain

Explore trails through ancient oak groves to breathtaking summit views

🔭

Sky Island Interpretive Trail

VMF's signature trail through an ancient oak, pine and cedar grove. Use the Sky Island Pipe Scope at the peak to see coastal and inland watersheds as far as Mexico.

  • 📍 Volcan Mountain, Julian CA
  • 🌲 Ancient oak woodland habitat
  • 🔭 Pipe Scope panoramic views to Mexico
Plan a Visit
🏕️

Volcan Mountain Nature Center

Near the headwaters of Santa Ysabel Creek. The Nature Center is VMF's hub for Education-Outreach and field programs — emphasizing outdoor discovery for all ages.

  • 📍 Near Santa Ysabel Creek headwaters
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Open to families & school groups
  • 🌱 Hands-on restoration activities
Book a Visit

Trail Maps & Conditions

Volcan Mountain rises over 5,000 feet above the Julian backcountry, offering trails through chaparral, oak woodland, and pine forest with panoramic views of San Diego County.

  • ⛰️ Elevation: 5,000+ feet
  • 📍 Julian, CA 92036
  • 🌄 Views to the Pacific Coast
